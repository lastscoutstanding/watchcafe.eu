Friday, April 3th 2026
The first Watch Cafe at Sioux Labs was a success! We had a nice turnout and enjoyed many fun conversations about watch types and the unique stories behind them. We opened up several timepieces to get a closer look at the movements. For many, it was their first time seeing the intricate mechanical parts and functions up close. We also put a few watches on the timegrapher, which provided some revealing data on their overall condition and accuracy.
We're already looking forward to the next one! Keep an eye on this page and the Sioux Intranet timeline for the next date. See you there!
